<p><strong>The Magnetic Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Magnetic Encoder</li><li>Absolute Magnetic Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic encoder market is primarily divided into two types: incremental and absolute magnetic encoders. Incremental magnetic encoders track the position changes relative to a reference point, providing information on motion by generating pulses as the shaft rotates. In contrast, absolute magnetic encoders provide a unique position value for every shaft angle, retaining positional information even during power loss. Both types are widely used in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, enabling precise motion control and feedback.</p></p>
