<p><p>The potato digger market consists of two main types: mounted and trailed potato diggers. Mounted potato diggers are attached directly to the tractor, allowing for compact design and enhanced maneuverability in smaller fields. They are ideal for small to medium-sized farms. In contrast, trailed potato diggers are towed behind the tractor, offering greater digging capacity suitable for larger fields. Each type caters to different agricultural needs, optimizing efficiency in potato harvesting based on farm size and terrain.</p></p>
