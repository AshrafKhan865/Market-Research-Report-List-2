<p><p>The handheld explosives detectors market consists of two primary types: trace detection and steam detection. Trace detection involves identifying minute traces of explosive materials on surfaces or in the air, often utilizing advanced imaging or chemical analysis technologies. Steam detection, on the other hand, uses vapor extraction methods to capture and analyze particles emitted from explosives. Both technologies are critical in enhancing security at airports, borders, and high-risk areas, providing quick and effective means to detect potential threats.</p></p>
