<p><strong>The Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption-type</li><li>Reflection-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Goggles can be categorized into absorption-type and reflection-type markets based on how they interact with light. Absorption-type goggles are designed to absorb certain wavelengths of light, often used for specific conditions like glare reduction in water sports. In contrast, reflection-type goggles utilize reflective coatings to bounce back harmful rays, making them ideal for environments with intense sunlight, such as skiing or snowboarding. Both types serve distinct purposes in protecting the eyes while enhancing visibility under varying conditions.</p></p>
