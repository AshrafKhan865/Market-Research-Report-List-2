<p><p>Spark Plasma Sintering (SPS) is an advanced sintering technique that utilizes pulsed electrical currents and mechanically induced pressure to consolidate materials, particularly powders, into dense and durable solid forms. This method is renowned for its ability to achieve rapid sintering at lower temperatures compared to traditional techniques, leading to finer microstructures and enhanced material properties. It is widely applied in various industries, including ceramics, metals, and composites.</p><p>The Spark Plasma Sintering market is projected to experience significant growth, expanding at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
