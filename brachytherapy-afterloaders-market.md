<p><p>Brachytherapy afterloaders are devices used in cancer treatment to deliver localized radiation therapy. The primary types include High-Dose Rate (HDR) afterloaders, which deliver a concentrated dose of radiation over a short time, allowing for precise targeting of tumors while minimizing exposure to surrounding healthy tissue. On the other hand, Pulsed-Dose Rate (PDR) afterloaders administer radiation in a series of short pulses, offering a balance between continuous treatment and localized effects, making them suitable for various tumor types.</p></p>
