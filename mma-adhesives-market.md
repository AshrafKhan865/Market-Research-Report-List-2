<p><strong>The MMA Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Adhesives</li><li>Thermoplasticity Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MMA adhesives market comprises two main types: thermosetting and thermoplastic adhesives. Thermosetting adhesives, once cured, create a strong, permanent bond and are resistant to heat and solvents, making them ideal for demanding applications. In contrast, thermoplastic adhesives can be remelted and reshaped, allowing for flexibility and reusability; they are suitable for applications requiring temporary bonding or easy disassembly. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction, each requiring specific bonding properties and performance capabilities.</p></p>
