<p><ul><li>Mineral Lubricant</li><li>Synthetic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic lubricant market is primarily divided into two types: mineral lubricants and synthetic lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from crude oil and are commonly used due to their cost-effectiveness and availability. They offer good lubrication properties but may degrade over time. Synthetic lubricants, on the other hand, are artificially formulated for enhanced performance, providing better thermal stability, reduced viscosity changes, and longer service life, making them suitable for demanding environments where high efficiency and reliability are essential.</p></p>
