<p><p>The tea dryers market encompasses various types of drying technologies, primarily including multi-stage fluid bed dryers and vacuum dryers. Multi-stage fluid bed dryers use a continuous process to efficiently remove moisture by circulating hot air through layers of tea leaves, ensuring uniform drying. Vacuum dryers operate under reduced pressure, enabling lower-temperature drying that preserves flavor and nutrients. Additionally, other drying methods such as rotary dryers and drum dryers contribute to the market, offering diverse options for optimizing tea processing.</p></p>
