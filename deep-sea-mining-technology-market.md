<p><p>Deep sea mining technology encompasses the extraction of valuable minerals from oceanic deposits. Key market types include cobalt-rich crusts, found on rocky formations and rich in cobalt and rare earth elements; manganese nodules, which are potato-shaped aggregates on the seafloor containing nickel, copper, and manganese; and seafloor massive sulfides, associated with hydrothermal vents, rich in copper, gold, and silver. Each type presents unique extraction challenges and opportunities, catering to the growing demand for critical minerals for technology and energy applications.</p></p>
