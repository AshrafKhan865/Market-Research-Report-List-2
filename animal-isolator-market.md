<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Isolator</li><li>Negative Pressure Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Animal Isolator market comprises primarily two types: positive pressure isolators and negative pressure isolators. Positive pressure isolators maintain a higher internal pressure than the external environment, preventing contaminants from entering and ensuring the safety of sensitive animals. In contrast, negative pressure isolators create a lower internal pressure, effectively containing any pathogens and protecting external environments. Both isolators are crucial for research and veterinary applications, ensuring animal welfare while minimizing contamination in biomedical and scientific studies.</p></p>
