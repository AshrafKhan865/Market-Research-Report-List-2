<p><strong>The Flange Nut Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Flange Nut</li><li>Serrated Flange Nut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flange nut market comprises two primary types: flat flange nuts and serrated flange nuts. Flat flange nuts possess a smooth, broad bearing surface that distributes load evenly, enhancing stability and reducing the risk of loosening. In contrast, serrated flange nuts feature a toothed or serrated surface that provides superior grip and vibration resistance, making them ideal for high-stress applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive, construction, and machinery, where secure fastening solutions are critical.</p></p>
