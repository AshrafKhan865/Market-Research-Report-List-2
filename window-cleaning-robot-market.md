<p><ul><li>Vacuum Suction</li><li>Fan Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The window cleaning robot market features two primary types: vacuum suction and fan adsorption. Vacuum suction robots utilize a powerful motor to create a suction force, securely adhering to the glass surface while effectively removing dirt and grime. In contrast, fan adsorption robots rely on high-velocity fans that generate airflow to cling to windows, allowing for thorough cleaning without leaving residue. Both technologies offer efficient solutions for automated window cleaning, catering to different preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
