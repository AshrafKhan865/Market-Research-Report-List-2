CMOS cameras are known for their lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them increasingly popular for real-time applications. In contrast, CCD cameras offer superior image quality and dynamic range, making them suitable for environments with varying lighting conditions. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle identification and surveillance systems.</p></p>
