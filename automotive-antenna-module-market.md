<p><ul><li>Fin Type</li><li>Rod Type</li><li>Screen Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive antenna module market comprises three primary types: Fin Type, Rod Type, and Screen Type. Fin Type antennas are often integrated into the vehicle's design, providing a sleek appearance while offering good reception. Rod Type antennas are traditional and extend outward, effectively capturing signals but can be less aerodynamic. Screen Type antennas utilize the vehicle's body or window surface to receive signals, allowing for innovative designs without protruding components. Each type serves distinct aesthetic and functional purposes within automotive design.</p></p>
