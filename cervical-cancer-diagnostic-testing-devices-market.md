<p><ul><li>Cytopathological Method</li><li>Molecular Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cervical cancer diagnostic testing devices market includes various types of methods. The cytopathological method focuses on microscopic examination of cell samples, typically obtained via Pap smears, to detect cellular abnormalities indicative of cervical cancer. In contrast, the molecular method utilizes advanced techniques such as PCR and HPV DNA testing to identify specific genetic material associated with cancer-causing strains of the Human Papillomavirus (HPV). Both methods play critical roles in early detection and management of cervical cancer.</p></p>
