<p><p>Lead foil tape is categorized into two main types based on adhesive: acrylic and rubber. Acrylic lead foil tape offers excellent UV resistance and long-term durability, making it suitable for outdoor applications and environments requiring stability over time. In contrast, rubber lead foil tape provides strong immediate adhesion and flexibility, making it ideal for indoor projects and surfaces requiring a conformable bond. Both types are widely used in various industries, including construction, electronics, and radiation shielding.</p></p>
