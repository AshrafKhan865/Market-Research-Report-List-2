<p><p>The organoclay powder market comprises three key types based on polarity: Low Polarity (Type I), Medium Polarity (Type II), and High Polarity (Type III). Low Polarity organoclays are effective in non-polar applications, primarily utilized in coatings and plastics. Medium Polarity organoclays balance hydrophilic and hydrophobic properties, making them ideal for various formulations like adhesives and sealants. High Polarity organoclays, with strong affinities for water, are predominantly used in applications such as drilling fluids and personal care products, providing enhanced performance in polar environments.</p></p>
