<p><p>Model Based Manufacturing Technologies encompass various systems that enhance production efficiency. MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems) focus on real-time monitoring and control of manufacturing processes. ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems integrate all facets of business operations, including finance and supply chain. MRP (Material Requirements Planning) systems concentrate on inventory management and scheduling. CAD (Computer-Aided Design) systems assist in product design and engineering. Other MbM software may include simulation tools and analysis platforms, broadening the capabilities of manufacturing processes.</p></p>
