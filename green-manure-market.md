<p><p>The green manure market is divided into two main types: leguminous and non-leguminous green manure. Leguminous green manure, such as clover and vetch, enriches soil with nitrogen through nitrogen-fixing bacteria, improving fertility and structure. Non-leguminous green manure, like rye and buckwheat, primarily enhances soil organic matter and suppresses weeds. Both types play vital roles in sustainable agriculture by promoting soil health, improving moisture retention, and enhancing crop yields, catering to eco-conscious farmers seeking natural soil enhancement methods.</p></p>
