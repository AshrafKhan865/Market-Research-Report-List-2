<p><strong>The Water Mist Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pipe System</li><li>Dry Pipe System</li><li>Deluge System</li><li>Preaction System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Mist Sprinkler Market encompasses various systems including Wet Pipe, Dry Pipe, Deluge, and Preaction systems. Wet Pipe systems are filled with water, providing immediate activation for fire response. Dry Pipe systems contain pressurized air, releasing water only when a fire is detected, ideal for cold environments. Deluge systems discharge water simultaneously across multiple nozzles, suitable for high-hazard areas. Preaction systems combine elements of wet and dry pipes, requiring activation from a fire detection system before water is released, enhancing control and safety.</p></p>
