<p><strong>The Industrial LiDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid State Lidar</li><li>Mechanical/Scanning Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial LiDAR market consists of two primary types: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR. Solid State LiDAR utilizes fixed optics and does not have moving parts, offering durability and compactness, making it ideal for automation and robotics. In contrast, Mechanical/Scanning LiDAR employs rotating mirrors to capture 360-degree views, allowing for detailed mapping and high-resolution data collection. Each type serves specific applications, from autonomous vehicles to construction and manufacturing, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
